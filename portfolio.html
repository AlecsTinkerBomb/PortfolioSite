My name is Alec Wright, an undergraduate architecture student at the Fay Jones School of Architecture and Design, University of Arkansas.

I'm drawn to the idea that buildings are one of the few things humans make that outlast them. Since childhood, I've been pulled toward creating and building, taking something imagined and making it real. Architecture, to me, is the discipline where craft, detail, and meaning come together, and it's what I'm constantly pushing myself to get better at.

My work spans digital modeling, visualization, and design documentation built with Rhino 8, Revit, Lumion, Enscape, and the Adobe Creative Suite. This site documents my work as I move through the program, a running record of how my thinking and skills evolve.
